About Me

I am a young woman, who finds it exciting to work with graphic design and code. I am inquisitive with regards to various tools. I am currently working with vector graphics, prototyping, HTML 5, CSS 3 and have been introduced to web responsive design, javascript and jQuery plugins. In addition, I also work with project planning.
I work best under structured conditions and gladly in teams. I like to try different things to challenge myself and have varied types of work tasks.
I study media design at CPH Business Academy Lyngby, which is a Danish education, so all my documentation is in Danish.

Movies: I am preferential to action and adventure movies, and I've loved Star Wars since I was four years old. But I am open to all genres.

Music: I have a broad taste in music, but the favourites are classic rock and classical music. I especially love classic rock played with a classical orchestra.
Video games: My favourite genre is role-playing and action-adventure.
Museums: I have traveled most of the world with my family, and I enjoy exploring other cultures and their history.
